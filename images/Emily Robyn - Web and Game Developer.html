Jots

Co-Parent

The nuclear family of the 1950s is becoming less and less common, but resources for these alternative family styles are few and far between. Co-Parent is a social network for collaborative parenting. It uses compatibility scores to help parents find others with smiliar values and provides a space for sharing resources.
Technologies: HTML5/CSS3, Ruby on Rails, Material Design. -Github-
